Our services

Health Care

At Amlica, we are dedicated to providing unparalleled healthcare assistance services that prioritize the well-being and comfort of individuals and their families. Our team of highly qualified, caring and compassionate professionals is committed to delivering personalized care tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Domestic Cleaners

We understand that a clean and tidy home is the foundation of a healthy and happy lifestyle. Our dedicated team of domestic cleaners is committed to thorough and efficient general cleaning. From dusting and vacuuming to sweeping and mopping, we cover every corner to ensure your home is spotless.

Housekeeping

Amlica Housekeepers ensures your home is a sanctuary of cleanliness and tranquility. Our dedicated team offers comprehensive services, from kitchen brilliance to bathroom care. With eco-friendly practices, trustworthiness, and flexible scheduling, we tailor our approach to our cunique needs.

Health Care training

We offer a range of online and practical training courses designed for healthcare assistants and support workers. Reach out to us to access free online training, and enroll in our paid mandatory courses valid for a one-year period.

Resume Review

Our team analyses your CV, offering expert recommendations to showcase your expertise to ensure your CV stands out. For candidates without a CV, we create one tailored to your strengths and experience.

Disclosure and Barring Service ( DBS )

In order to ensure compliance, we ask for an enhanced and Updated DBS check. Obtain a complimentary check and submit your application for a DBS with us to receive a copy of your criminal record for a fee.
